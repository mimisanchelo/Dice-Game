Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled or the player decides to "Hold Points":
If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn.
If the player rolls any other number, it is added to their current total turn and the player's turn continues.
If a player chooses to "Hold Points", their current total turn is added to their score, and it becomes the next player's turn.
The first player to score 100 or more points wins.
